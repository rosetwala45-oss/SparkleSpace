Our Story

Founded in 2016, SparkleSpace began with a simple mission: to help people reclaim their time and enjoy cleaner, more organized spaces. What started as a small team of dedicated cleaners has grown into a full-service cleaning and organization company.

We understand that a clean, organized home isn't just about aesthetics—it's about creating a peaceful environment where you can thrive. Our team of experienced professionals brings expertise, reliability, and a genuine passion for transformation to every project.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of satisfied clients across the region, offering everything from deep cleaning services to complete home organization solutions.
